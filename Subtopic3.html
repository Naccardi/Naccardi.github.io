The Cost-Effectiveness of Buying a PC Online



URL
 Online PC Budgeting
Site Author
 Geoff Williams
Abstract
 This site focuses on the budgeting aspect of PC purchasing, mentioning several alternatives to saving money when purchasing a PC online. The site starts by labeling the common ways that purchasers can save money online such as holidays or other special online days such as Cyber Monday or Amazon Prime Day deals. Regardless of these special occasions, the site makes sure to reestablish the purpose why an individual purchases a PC to begin with, using this as a tool for purchasers to save money. The site briefly mentions when and when not to cheap out when buying PCs, and on one occasion mentions the idea of buying refurbished or open-box PCs that are commonly found on e-commerce sites.



URL
 Cost-Effectivness of Buying vs. Building a PC
Site Author
 CDW LLC
Abstract
 This site offers an alternative to saving money by offering a comparison between purchasing a pre-built PC and purchasing each component of a PC online instead. This site assumes that the purchaser is interested in purchasing a desktop computer, meaning components such as a case or monitor would be additionally required. The site demonstrates that although a pre-built PC is cheaper up-front compared to buying each component, building a PC will result in overall better build quality, in turn leading to a longer lifespan and saving the purchaser money. By buying each component, the purchaser also is informed of each component and their capabilities, while also having the ability to repair a PC themselves.
