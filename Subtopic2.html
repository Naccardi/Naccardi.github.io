 Pros and Cons of Online PC Purchasing
Site Author
 Staff Writer
Abstract
 This site demonstrates the pros and cons of purchasing a PC online to help purchasers make informed decisions. Writer lays out the article by including convenience, accessibility, competitive pricing, product information, and reviews as advantages of PC purchasing online. He focuses on the idea that online retailing websites provide a feeling of no pressure and allows purchasers to not only have 24/7 availability but allows purchasers to have more variety in the types of PCs and the specifications they are looking for when making a purchase. The article also includes the idea of user reviews playing an important role in online PC purchasing so individuals are given information about the PC to have a more accurate understanding of its capabilities.

 Comparison between Online and In Person PC Purchasing
Site Author
 Eric Griffith
Abstract
 Although the site's main purpose is to identify the best online or in-person stores to purchase a PC from, the site gathers information from purchasers via surveys to determine the benefits of buying a PC online. The article describes the desire for most brick and mortar stores to have e-commerce as an option for purchasers as purchasers were often satisfied with the user product review and product description aspects of the e-commerce sites and several other factors. The site demonstrates through measurement of purchasing satisfaction that oftentimes, stores with the ability to purchase or at least view a PC online generally have a higher satisfaction rating.
