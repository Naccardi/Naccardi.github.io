The Purpose of Buying a PC Online


URL
Cheap vs. Expensive PC Purchasing
Site Author
 Sven Bauduin


Abstract
 The site contains essential information about purchasing a PC online, labeling the differences between cheap and expensive personal computers and their worth. It mentions the differences in PC capabilities through the essential components of a PC, and how these components impact their performance. The site starts with the processing power of a PC, mentioning the number of cores a CPU might have as a starting point as to what PC a user may want to purchase. The website mentions all essential components of a PC, including CPU, GPU, RAM, PSU, storage, and a few other components to demonstrate the complexity of a PC and how one user may prefer higher-end specifications, while another user may prefer lower-end specifications, especially on a lower budget.



URL
 Types of PCs and Their Differences
Site Author
 Lenovo
Abstract
 This site is another site that provides information about PC specifications, but it makes a differentiation between certain types of PCs, noting that a PC can be a desktop, laptop, netbook, or all-in-one which is described as a desktop computer that combines the monitor and the computer into a single device. The site serves as a glossary for PCs and is created by Lenovo to help individuals decide what type of PC or specifications are best for them by asking common questions about PCs and their essential components. These questions help guide the purchaser when trying to find their purpose or justification for buying a PC online. This site also labels the advantages and disadvantages of PC ownership.
